Our Story

At Organic ME we believe going organic is the next step in human evolution, it's healthier, more accessible and cheaper, but you didn't come to this page to learn about organic foods, you came to learn about us! Established in 2017, a smart mind with nothing but a small plot in land saw an opportunity in the market place to start something exciting, and got in contact with a few others who shared similar interests and together they created something amazing. Today Organic ME remains true to how this company was created, by being local and supporting the local environment and people. We at Organic ME also believe in sticking to our morals and what we believe in, which is organic of course!
